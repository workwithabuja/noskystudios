About the company

Creative foundations. Technology-led growth.

NoSky Studios Ltd develops digital products and produces creative media for modern users, creators, businesses, and organisations.

Our evolution

From multimedia production to creative technology.

NoSky Studios Ltd is a Nigerian creative technology company. The business began with photography, videography, film production, event coverage, content creation, and content licensing. That media foundation remains an active part of the company.

The company has expanded into software development, mobile application development, productivity tools, digital products, app subscriptions, in-app purchases, and practical technology solutions. This expansion reflects one connected direction: designing useful experiences through both technology and media.

What we do

Two capabilities, one company.

Software & Products

Mobile applications, productivity tools, digital platforms, subscriptions, in-app purchases, and technology solutions.

Creative Media

Photography, videography, film production, event coverage, branded content, post-production, and content licensing.

Thoughtful Execution

Clear planning, intuitive design, strong production, and careful delivery guide every product and media project.

Current product

LifeEngine

LifeEngine is a productivity application currently being developed by NoSky Studios Ltd. It is designed to help users manage schedules, habits, subscriptions, routines, tasks, and daily activities through a modern and intuitive digital experience.

LifeEngine represents the company's continuing investment in owned software products and practical technology for everyday life.
