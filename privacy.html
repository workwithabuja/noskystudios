Privacy Policy

This page explains the basic privacy approach for NoSky Studios and noskystudios.ng.

Overview

NoSky Studios respects your privacy. This website is designed as a lightweight static site and does not require user accounts to browse.

Information you provide

If you contact us through a form or future email integration, we may receive your name, email address, area of interest, and message so we can respond to your inquiry.

Website data

Static hosting providers may collect basic technical logs such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps for security and performance purposes.

Cookies

This website does not intentionally set tracking cookies. If analytics or embedded services are added later, this policy should be updated.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the website, respond to inquiries, comply with law, or protect the studio.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact NoSky Studios through noskystudios.ng.
